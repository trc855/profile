Roots

Roots is a simple, cute garden tracker and management app my friends and I made as our final project. We were quite limited by time and expertise but I am proud of the team and the final result.

I really enjoyed putting into practice what I had learned about databasing, schema design, migrations, and the MVC pattern. Using modals for all our forms presented new challenges, which required me to extend myself and build on what I had been taught. Although not my wheelhouse, I spent a lot of time on frontend in an effort become more well rounded. The "Add Crop Form", "Notification Worm", "Helpful Hints" and Info dropdowns are the frontend contributions I am most proud of.

Big shout out to Claire for having a vision, taking the lead, and putting in a superhuman effort. Also big respect to Archie for the amazing 3D!
